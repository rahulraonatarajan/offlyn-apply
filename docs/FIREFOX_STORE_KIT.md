### Description (Full)

```markdown
## Auto-Fill Job Applications in Seconds with 100% Local AI

Offlyn Apply is a privacy-first job application assistant that helps you fill out applications faster, track your progress, and generate custom cover letters—all without sending your data to the cloud.

### 🚀 Key Features

**⚡ Instant Auto-Fill**
Automatically detect and fill job application forms with your information. Works on LinkedIn, Indeed, company career pages, and more.

**📊 Application Dashboard**
Track all your applications in one place with a beautiful Kanban board. See which applications are in progress, interviewing, or closed.

**✍️ AI Cover Letters**
Generate personalized cover letters tailored to each job description. Powered by local AI (Ollama) - no cloud required.

**🔒 100% Private**
Your resume, profile, and application data never leave your device. No remote servers, no tracking, no data collection.

**🧠 Smart Learning**
The extension learns from your inputs to improve autofill accuracy over time—completely offline.

### Why Choose Offlyn Apply?

✓ **Privacy First** - Your data stays on your device  
✓ **Fast Setup** - Configure your profile once, apply everywhere  
✓ **Cross-Platform** - Works on LinkedIn, Indeed, Workday, Greenhouse, and custom career pages  
✓ **Professional** - Built for job seekers who value their privacy and time  
✓ **Open Source** - Transparent, auditable code  

### How It Works

1. **Install & Set Up**: Add your profile information once  
2. **Navigate to Job**: Visit any job application page  
3. **Click Auto-Fill**: Offlyn Apply detects fields and fills them instantly  
4. **Review & Submit**: Verify the information and submit your application  
5. **Track Progress**: View all applications in your personal dashboard  

### Technical Details

**Local AI Support**:  
Connect Ollama for advanced features like cover letter generation and resume optimization. All AI processing happens on your device.

**Supported Sites**:
- LinkedIn
- Indeed
- Glassdoor
- Monster
- ZipRecruiter
- Company career pages (Workday, Greenhouse, Lever, etc.)

**Privacy Guarantee**:  
Offlyn Apply does NOT:
- Send data to remote servers
- Track your activity
- Store data in the cloud
- Require account creation
- Collect analytics

### Requirements

- Firefox 109 or later
- Optional: Ollama (for AI features)

### Support & Feedback

Questions? Issues? Feature requests?  
Visit our GitHub repository or email support@offlyn.ai

---

**Made with ❤️ by Offlyn.ai**  
Privacy-first productivity tools for modern professionals.
```

## 6. Copy Guidelines

### Voice & Tone
- **Professional** but not corporate
- **Confident** but not arrogant
- **Technical** but not jargony
- **Reassuring** (addresses privacy concerns)

### Key Messages
1. **Privacy is paramount** - "Your data never leaves your device"
2. **Speed matters** - "Seconds, not minutes"
3. **Built for trust** - "Open source, auditable"
4. **Works everywhere** - "LinkedIn, Indeed, and beyond"

### What NOT to Say
❌ "AI-powered" without "local" qualifier (sounds cloud-based)  
❌ "Store your data" (sounds like cloud storage)  
❌ "Upload your resume" without "locally" (sounds like server upload)  
❌ Comparisons to competitors by name  
❌ Unrealistic claims ("100% accurate")
