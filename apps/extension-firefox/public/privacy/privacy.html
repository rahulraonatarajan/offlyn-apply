Privacy Policy

How Offlyn Apply handles your data

The Short Version

Your data never leaves your device. Offlyn Apply stores everything locally in your browser and makes zero network requests to external servers.

100% Local No Accounts

What Data We Store

  • Your profile - Name, email, phone, work history, education (entered by you during onboarding)
  • Job applications - Company, position, date, status (recorded when you submit applications)
  • Learned values - Corrections you make to autofill suggestions, so the extension improves over time
  • Extension settings - Your preferences (enabled/disabled, dry run mode, etc.)

All data is stored in browser.storage.local on your device. It is never transmitted anywhere.

What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not collect analytics or telemetry
  • We do not track your browsing history
  • We do not send data to any server (ours or third-party)
  • We do not require an account or login
  • We do not use cookies or fingerprinting
  • We do not inject ads or affiliate links

AI Features (Ollama)

Offlyn Apply uses Ollama for AI-powered features like resume parsing and cover letter generation. Ollama runs locally on your computer - your data stays on your machine.

  • Ollama communicates via a local native messaging bridge (localhost:11434)
  • No data is sent to OpenAI, Google, or any cloud AI service
  • If Ollama is not installed, AI features are simply disabled

Permissions Explained

Here's why the extension requests each permission:

Permission Why It's Needed
storage Save your profile, settings, and application history locally
tabs Open the dashboard and onboarding pages in new tabs
<all_urls> Detect job application pages and autofill forms on any website
localhost:11434 Communicate with your local Ollama AI server (never leaves your machine)
menus Add helpful options to the right-click context menu

Data Deletion

You have full control over your data at all times:

  • Settings page - Reset Self-ID data or clear all applications
  • Onboarding page - Delete learned values individually
  • Uninstall - Removing the extension deletes all stored data automatically

Third-Party Services

The dashboard uses Chart.js (loaded from cdn.jsdelivr.net) for rendering charts. This is the only external resource loaded. Chart.js does not collect or transmit any user data.
