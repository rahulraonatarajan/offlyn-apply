Help & Tutorial

Learn how to get the most out of Offlyn Apply

Quick Start Guide

1
Set Up Your Profile
Click the extension icon and select Manage Profile. Upload your resume or manually enter your details (name, email, phone, work history).
Tip: Uploading a resume auto-fills most fields for you.
2
Visit a Job Application Page
Navigate to any job application form (LinkedIn, Indeed, Greenhouse, Lever, etc.). The extension automatically detects application pages and shows a small floating icon.
3
Auto-Fill the Form
Click Auto-Fill in the popup or on the floating panel. The extension fills in detected fields using your profile data.
Tip: Use Dry Run mode (in Settings) to preview what would be filled without changing the form.
4
Review and Submit
Review the filled fields, make any adjustments, and submit your application. Offlyn Apply learns from your corrections to improve future suggestions.
5
Track Your Progress
Open the Dashboard to see all your applications in a Kanban board. Drag cards between columns to update their status (Submitted, Interviewing, Accepted, etc.).

Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't the extension detecting my job page?▼
The extension uses heuristics to detect job application pages. It looks for form fields, apply buttons, and known ATS platforms (Greenhouse, Lever, etc.). If a page isn't detected, it may not have enough signals. Try clicking "Auto-Fill" manually from the popup.
What is Ollama and do I need it?▼
Ollama is a local AI runtime that runs language models on your computer. It's needed for advanced features like resume parsing and cover letter generation. Basic autofill works without it. Install Ollama from ollama.com and the native host bridge from the extension's native-host folder.
Is my data sent to any server?▼
No. All your data (profile, applications, learned values) is stored in your browser's local storage. AI features use Ollama which runs on your own computer. The only external resource loaded is Chart.js from a CDN for the dashboard charts.
How do I update my profile?▼
Click the extension icon, then "Manage Profile". This opens the onboarding page where you can update any of your personal details, work history, or cover letter preferences.
What is Dry Run mode?▼
Dry Run mode lets you preview what would be filled in without actually changing any form values. It's useful for testing the extension on a new site. Enable it in Settings or from the popup's Advanced section.
How do I delete my data?▼
Go to Settings to clear application history or reset Self-ID data. To remove everything, simply uninstall the extension - all data is deleted automatically since it's stored only in your browser.
Can I export my applications?▼
Yes! Open the Dashboard and click "Export CSV" to download all your tracked applications as a spreadsheet file. You can also copy your profile data from Settings using "Export Profile".
