Everything You Need to Land Your Next Role

Offlyn Apply handles the repetitive parts of job hunting so you can focus on what actually matters.

Smart Auto-Fill

Detects all form fields and fills them instantly from your profile. Works on Workday, Greenhouse, Lever, and plain HTML forms.

AI Cover Letters

Generate tailored cover letters using a local Ollama model. Professional output, tuned to each job — never sent to any third party.

Application Tracker

Kanban-style dashboard to track every application from submitted to offer. All stored locally in your browser.

100% Private

All profile data and history live in your browser's local storage. No backend, no cloud, no analytics. What we can't see, we can't sell.

ATS Detection

Automatically identifies job pages and application systems. Shows a compatibility score so you know how strong a match you are before applying.

Learns From You

Reinforcement learning from your manual corrections. The more you use it, the smarter the autofill gets for your specific profile.

Privacy First Architecture

Your Data Stays Yours

Unlike most job tools that funnel your resume and personal info through their servers, Offlyn Apply never sends your data anywhere. Everything — your profile, applications, AI results — lives in your browser.

  • Local storage only

    All data stored in browser.storage.local — never synced to any cloud

  • No backend

    Zero servers, zero databases. The only network requests go to localhost (Ollama)

  • Local AI with Ollama

    Cover letters generated on your machine — your resume never touches OpenAI, Anthropic, or any external model

  • Open source & auditable

    Every line of code is public. Don't trust us — verify it yourself

Zero Data Collection

We built it this way on purpose. The job market is stressful enough — you shouldn't have to worry about who has your resume.

  • ✓ No account creation
  • ✓ No email collection
  • ✓ No analytics or tracking
  • ✓ No cookies or fingerprinting
  • ✓ No data sold to third parties

Because we can't access what we never collect.
